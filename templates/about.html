About PresentSer

PresentSer is a simple, fast, student-friendly attendance calculator built by Fayas. It helps students track their attendance with zero stress — clean UI, quick results, and no ads.

We all joke about bunking, but the real problem begins when attendance drops below the required percentage and we start worrying about condonation. Every semester, students stress about the same questions

The idea is simple: give students a clean tool that actually helps them stay on track. No confusion, no clutter — just the numbers you need.

questions:

“How many more days do I need to attend to avoid condonation?”
“If I take leave tomorrow, will it go below the safe limit?”
“What exactly is my current percentage right now?”

Most apps online were complicated, filled with ads, or gave incorrect results. So I built PresentSer — a clean, fast, and distraction-free attendance tool that tells you exactly:
  • ✔ How much more attendance you need to stay safe
  • ✔ Whether you’re close to getting condonation
  • ✔ How many days you can safely bunk (if any)

No ads. No login. No unnecessary screens. Just the numbers you need to stay within college rules — without stress.

PresentSer was created because every student deserves clarity, not confusion, especially when attendance decides whether you write exams.
